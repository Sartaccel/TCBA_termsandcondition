1. Introduction

These Terms & Conditions govern the use of the TCBA Mobile Application operated for Tuticorin Container Brokers Association (TCBA). The application facilitates container movement processing, stakeholder approvals, wallet management, and secure digital payments between registered port stakeholders.

By accessing or using this App, you agree to be bound by these Terms. If you do not agree, you must discontinue use immediately.

2. Eligibility

The App is intended strictly for registered business stakeholders including:

  • Customs House Agents (CHA)
  • Container Freight Stations (CFS)
  • Liners
  • Container Yards (CY)
  • Exporters and Importers

Accounts are created through the TCBA Web Admin system. Public self-registration is not available.

3. User Accounts & Authentication

  • Login credentials are issued after stakeholder registration.
  • Password reset is mandatory during first login.
  • Users must set an MPIN for secure access.
  • Fingerprint authentication is optional and processed locally on the device.
  • After three failed login attempts, the account may be temporarily locked.

Users are responsible for safeguarding login credentials and ensuring authorized usage.

4. Scope of Services

4.1 Container Movement Processing

  • Upload or manual entry of CDO/CRO details
  • Container number validation
  • Submission to YARD systems via API
  • Approval or rejection workflows

4.2 Wallet & Payment Management

  • Wallet creation and balance management
  • Wallet top-up
  • Instant and Credit payment options
  • Automated payout processing
  • Ledger tracking and reconciliation

4.3 Notifications

  • Payment request alerts
  • Container approval notifications
  • Low wallet balance alerts

5. Payment Terms

Payment modes may include:

  • Instant
  • Credit
  • Instant & Credit

Before processing payments, the system validates:

  • Transaction amount accuracy
  • Beneficiary verification
  • Duplicate transaction detection
  • Fraud monitoring
  • Idempotency enforcement

TCBA is not liable for delays caused by banking network issues, payment gateway downtime, regulatory restrictions, or force majeure events.

6. Wallet Usage

  • Payments are deducted upon approval.
  • Wallet balances are non-interest bearing.
  • All transactions are logged for audit and reconciliation.
